What is Strength Visualiser

Im sure you judge your strength growth by how much more weight you've lifted compared to the week before, but seeing the big picture is difficult. I mean sure, you can keep a log of the weight you lifted each week, but it will get long and messy. With the Strength Visualiser, we take all your lifting data and plot it into a graph that you can easily see your overall strength growth. We also allow you to store the data in our database for easy retreival.

How it works

Based on the data you provide to us like the weight lifted, the year and month it was done, we will sort the data and put it into a insightful line graph for you to view and you can take action based on the result or even celebrate your amazing progress!

Why you should create an account

With an account, you won't have to always key in your data as with an account, once you insert a entry, it will be saved in our secure database till you use it to draw out a chart. You also get features like filtering, which makes it easier for you to narrow the the graph data you wish to see allowing you to view your desired period.
